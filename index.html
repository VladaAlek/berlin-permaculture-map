The Problem is the Solution

Embark on a journey through our interactive map and uncover the vast array of permaculture projects flourishing in Berlin. Rooted in the foundational principles of Earth Care, People Care, and Fair Shares, each project tells a unique story of sustainable living and community collaboration.


Dive deep, be inspired, and consider adding your own touch. Together, we can grow this map into an even more comprehensive and dynamic reflection of Berlin's thriving permaculture scene.

Why Choose Permaculture?

At its core, permaculture is a transformative and regenerative solution to today's pressing ecological challenges. From rejuvenating soil fertility and championing water conservation to mitigating climate change and bolstering biodiversity, it's a holistic approach with tangible benefits for our planet.


But the magic of permaculture doesn't stop there. It also fosters a vibrant social environment, advocating for a healthier lifestyle, cooperative spirit, shared resources, and synergistic actions across communities.
